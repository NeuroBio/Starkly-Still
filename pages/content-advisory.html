This page exists as a last warning in case "thriller," "horror," and "supernatural" were not enough to make it clear what you are walking into.

  • I do not pull punches.
  • I will write graphic gore.
  • I will place immense psychological pressure on my characters.
  • I will subject my characters to all types of abuse on and off screen.
  • I will break relationships.
  • I will kill main characters.
  • I will kill pets.
  • I do not promise happy endings.

What I do promise is this:

  • Characters make bad decisions genuinely. It will be in their nature to make those mistakes.
  • I don't use heavy topics for shock factor. And I won't use them lightly.
  • By and large, it won't be the actual gore or abuse that hurts. It'll be what the characters do about it that hurts.
